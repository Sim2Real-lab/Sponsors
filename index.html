About NITK

National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK) Surathkal is one of India’s premier engineering institutions, renowned for academic excellence and innovation. With a legacy of producing top engineers, entrepreneurs, and leaders, NITK fosters research, creativity, and social impact.

NIRF has ranked NITK 17th among technical institutes and 2nd among NITs.

About Sim2Real

Sim2Real is a flagship hackathon by RoboTechNITK that bridges the gap between simulation and real-life applications. It's an event that challenges students to apply their skills in a competitive environment.

Past Success:

Successfully organised the hackathon during Engineer 2024 with more than 40 participants and a ₹30,000 prize pool.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate and aspiring engineers, whose primary objective is to build an autonomous vehicle capable of maneuvering. We strive to promote our university, partners, and sponsors at National and International levels in robotics challenges.
